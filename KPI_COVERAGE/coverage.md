## KPI - COVERAGE IN DAYS, STOCK VALUE
<img src="/KPI_COVERAGE/KPI_COV_1.png?raw=true"/>
Project description:

This KPI - COVERAGE IN WEEKS, STOCK VALUE dashboard may support inventroy planners to keep stock on the right level. 
Very simple visualtions focus on two main KPIs - value of inventory and coverage of current inventory in days. Based on these dashboards planner may asses very easily if stock's evolution proceeds in the right way and launch counter meaures if necessary.
